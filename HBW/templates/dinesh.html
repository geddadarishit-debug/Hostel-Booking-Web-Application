Project Overview

Our team developed a Hostel Booking System, an interface connecting hostel owners and customers. My role focused on creating an appealing and functional front-end.

My Contribution

HTML Development

I built the website structure with HTML5 and made it easy for customers and owners to use by adding forms for booking and sections to show hostel details. I used Jinja2 to loop through data and show it dynamically.

CSS

I created a stylesheet for the hostel booking system with a dark-gold theme and light mode option. I added animations, glowing effects, and hover styles to make it look modern. I styled forms, tables, and buttons to fit all screens.
